Szechuan House
★★★★★4.8
Chinese Szechuan 🌶 Spicy ✓ Reliable 🌙 Late Night ☁ Comfort
Notes

Great for late-night comfort food. Dry pot is very reliable — the lamb version is the standout. Dumplings have improved since they changed suppliers. The cold appetizers are hit or miss; stick with the cucumber salad. Service is fast but not particularly warm. Good for groups. Portions are generous for the price. Delivery packaging is adequate but some dishes travel better than others.
